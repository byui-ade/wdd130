Lagos

Lagos is a state in the south west part of Nigeria. Lagos State is the most populous city in Nigeria. Bounded to the south by the Bight of Benin and to the west by the international border with Benin, Lagos State share border with Ogun State. Ikeja is the state capital of Lagos State. Lagos State is called the center of excellence and is a major economic center of Nigeria. It would be the fifth largest economy in Africa.
